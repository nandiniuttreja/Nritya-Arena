What We Offer

Choreography

We are providing you with the studio and home classes in Muzaffarnagar (UP) and the online classes as per the convenience. Dancing with the friends in the academy, online classes for the comfort at your home and home tuitions are all fun while dancing.




About Us



Nritya Arena

From professional dancing to weddings, events or functions dances. Nritya Arena is here to provide the best quality dance training. We offer home classes and regular studio classes, here, in Muzaffarnagar. We also offer online interactive dance sessions. Learn dancing while having fun with our professional dancers and trainers. With various dance instructors, you can experience the quality dancing that will make you want to dance the whole day. The different types of dance styles are being offered here. Be it a Funky Bollywood or a Professional Hip-Hop Dance, we are here to make you ace any form. Gautam Bayal, the founder of Nritya Arena initiated to open a dance class, with a vision to make people happy by making them groove to their own rhythm. The ultimate aim of our dance studio is to provide everyone with the best facilities, so that they dance the stress out of their everyday routines.
